      Instructions

      Card Connections: Find the Link!

      • Start the game by clicking the "Start Game" button.
      • Two cards will be displayed: Card 1 and Card 2.
      • Your goal is to find a connection between the two cards by navigating through the sets of Card 1.
      • Click on a set listed under Card 1 to display the cards in that set.
      • Choose a card from the displayed set that shares a common set with Card 2.
      • If you've successfully found a connection, the game will congratulate you and show your performance.
      • To play again, click the "Start a New Game" button.

      Challenge yourself to find the connection between the two cards in the shortest amount of time and with the fewest number of sets!
